Some techniques will be selected for further study depending on the thesis's subject and student profile. At the end of the course the student should know to select the technique(s) more appropriated to solve a specific problem. Resultados de aprendizagem e competênciasIt is intended that the student acquires knowledge of physics experimental techniques, experience in some experimental techniques and develops their capacity for research work. ISBN: 978-0-444-82476-9 Métodos de ensino e atividades de aprendizagemTeaching will be tutorial. The students must perform all the tasks requested over the semester. The course will have a practical and laboratory component. In general three research studies will be asked, being the topics chosen according to the theme of the student's thesis. For each research study a written report and an oral presentation must be prepared.
